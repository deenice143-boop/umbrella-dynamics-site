2. Information we collect

  • Business and contact details you submit through our forms: business name, description, address, email, phone numbers, hours, and agent configuration preferences.
  • Payment information, processed entirely by Stripe — we never see or store full card numbers.
  • Call data: when our AI agent answers calls for your business, audio, transcripts, and call metadata (duration, timestamps, caller responses) are processed and stored by our voice platform provider (ElevenLabs) to deliver the service and generate call summaries.
  • Referral data: if you arrive through a partner link, a referral code may be stored in your browser to credit the referring partner.
  • Website basics: our site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may log standard technical data (IP address, browser type) per its own policies.

3. How we use information

  • Creating, operating, and tuning your AI receptionist and WhatsApp Business presence
  • Forwarding genuine calls and delivering summaries to you
  • Billing, account management, and support
  • Paying partner commissions on referred accounts
  • Improving our service quality

We do not sell personal information.

4. Service providers we share data with

To operate the service, data flows through: ElevenLabs (voice agents, call processing), Meta / WhatsApp Business Platform (messaging and call delivery), Stripe (payments), OpenRouter (AI-generated agent configuration), Render (application hosting), GitHub Pages (website hosting), FormSubmit (form delivery to our email), Proton AG (email), and Dub (partner link tracking). Each processes data under its own privacy policy.

Calls answered by our AI agents may be recorded, transcribed, and processed by automated systems. Businesses using our service are responsible for complying with call-recording consent laws applicable in their jurisdiction, including informing their callers where required.
