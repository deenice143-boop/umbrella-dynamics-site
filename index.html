How it works

Three steps. Ten minutes. Done.

STEP 1

Point your calls at us

Keep your existing business number. One simple forwarding setting sends incoming calls to your Umbrella agent — no new hardware, no new SIM, no app for your customers to learn.

STEP 2

The agent answers & screens

Every call is answered in two rings, in a natural voice with your business name. The agent asks what the call is about, spots robocalls and sales pitches instantly, and verifies real customers.

STEP 3

Real callers reach you

Genuine customers are forwarded straight to your phone with a heads-up on WhatsApp: who's calling and why. Everything else becomes a tidy message summary you can read when it suits you.
